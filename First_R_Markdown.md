Numeric Vector Manipulations
----------------------------

    age<-c(23,25,42,30,35)
    age

    ## [1] 23 25 42 30 35

Adding elements to the existing Vector
--------------------------------------

    age<-c(age,52)
    age

    ## [1] 23 25 42 30 35 52

Subsetting elements from the Vector
-----------------------------------

    age[3]

    ## [1] 42

    age[c(2,5)]

    ## [1] 25 35
